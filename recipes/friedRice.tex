\begin{recipe}{Euro Fried Rice}
    \begin{ingr}[1]
    \item Leftover rice
    \item Onion
    \item Carrot, maybe mushrooms
    \item Other leftover vegtables
    \item Egg
    \item Bay leave
    \item Pepper and salt
    \end{ingr}

    Fry eggs as you like - remove from pan. Fry onions, and
    potentially mushrooms,
    add rice and keep frying. Add vegtables, bay leave, salt and pepper,
    and put a lid on. Serve with eggs on top.
\end{recipe}
