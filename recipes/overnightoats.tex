\begin{recipe}{Overnight Oats}
    \begin{ingr}[1]
    \item 80 g of Oats
    \item 225 mL of water
    \item 30 g of protein powder
    \item 25 g og chocolate
    \item 25 g of dried fruit (berries, raisins or so)
    \item tablespoon of oil
    \end{ingr}

    Combine oats, water and sugar and let it rest in a cool place overnight.
    Mix with oil and protein powder and serve with chopped chocolate and fruit.

\end{recipe}
